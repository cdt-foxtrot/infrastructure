Did You Know?

Minecraft's Original Name

Before it was called "Minecraft," the game was first named "Cave Game" by its creator, Markus "Notch" Persson. It was later renamed "Minecraft: Order of the Stone" before finally becoming just "Minecraft"!

The Creeper Origin Story

The iconic Creeper was actually created by accident! Notch was trying to create a pig model but mixed up the height and length dimensions, resulting in a tall, strange-looking creature. He liked the design and turned it into the explosive mob we know today.

Endermen Language

The strange sounds that Endermen make are actually English words and phrases played backwards and pitched down! They say things like "hello," "what's up," and "look for the eye."

The Nether Scale

Every block you travel in the Nether is equivalent to 8 blocks in the Overworld. This makes Nether travel an efficient way to cover large distances!

More Minecraft Facts

World Size

A Minecraft world is approximately 8 times the surface area of Earth before it starts to break down! The technical limit is 60 million blocks in each direction from the center.

Minecraft Education

Minecraft: Education Edition is used in over 115 countries by teachers to teach subjects ranging from math and science to history and language arts.

Ghast Sounds

The eerie sounds made by Ghasts in the Nether are actually recordings of C418's (the game's original composer) cat when it was waking up from a nap!
